About

EcoSentinel AI

EcoSentinel AI delivers real-time environmental insights, alerts, and recommendations for Nairobi's informal settlements. It uses AI-powered predictive modeling and open datasets to monitor air quality, flood risks, water contamination, and climate patterns. Learn more
